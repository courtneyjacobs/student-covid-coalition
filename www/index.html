Our Mission

We are forming the Student COVID Coalition in recognition of the need for increased labor in order to operate a large institution, such as the University of Virginia. Operating large institutions in the midst of a pandemic may require increased cleaning shifts, regular testing and temperature checks, and the construction of temporary buildings, as well as any other tasks the University might require.


The SCC seeks to form a volunteer labor force composed of students in order to provide the University of Virginia with the labor necessary to operate in a safe manner on Grounds in the upcoming year. At this time, the SCC is soliciting formal commitments from students to provide a designated number of weekly hours in volunteer labor should the University require this aid. Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) will be provided to all laborers to ensure safety for our members. We have been in contact with student representatives and plan to coordinate our efforts with the University administration as a whole.
