Join us

To get involved, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Fill out the recruitment form

You can find our preliminary recruitment form here. Upon receipt of your interest in recruitment, we will reach out to you to seek your formal commitment.

Step 2: Make a commitment

You will receive an email with an offer to make a formal commitment to dedicate a certain number of weekly hours to the SCC. By signing this form, you will become an official member of the Student COVID Coalition.

Step 3: You're in

You will then be added to our listserv and official membership group, and be included in all of our workforce statistics as we continue to be in communication with University stakeholders.


CIOs: please use our CIO Partnership Form.
